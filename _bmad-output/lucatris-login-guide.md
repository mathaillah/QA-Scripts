# Lucatris.com User Login Guide

## Getting Started - 3 Easy Steps

### Step 1: Access the Website
- Open your web browser
- Navigate to: `https://lucatris.com`
- Wait for the page to load completely

### Step 2: Locate and Click Sign In  
- Find the **Sign In** button (typically in the top-right corner)
- Click on **Sign In**
- You'll be taken to the login page

### Step 3: Enter Your Login Details
- In the **Email** field, type your email address
- In the **Password** field, type your password
- Click the **Login** button (or press Enter)

**🎉 Welcome!** You are now successfully logged into your Lucatris account.
